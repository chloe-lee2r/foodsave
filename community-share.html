📋 Sharing guidelines

  • Food must be safe to eat and properly stored
  • Be honest about ingredients and expiry dates
  • Label any common allergens (nuts, dairy, gluten, etc.)
  • Include clear pickup instructions for your safety
  • Only share what you'd be happy to eat yourself!
