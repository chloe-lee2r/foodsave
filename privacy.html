Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how foodsavvi ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application and website (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Username and password — for account authentication
  • Email address — for account recovery (optional)
  • Phone number — for contact purposes (optional)

For business accounts, we additionally collect:

  • Business name, phone, email, and physical address
  • Business operating hours

2. Data Stored Locally

We use local storage on your device to save:

  • Shopping lists and items
  • Fridge items with expiry dates
  • User preferences and settings
  • Profile pictures

This data stays on your device and is synced to our secure cloud servers for backup.

3. Location Data

With your explicit permission, we access your device's location to:

  • Show nearby stores with food deals
  • Find community food shares in your area
  • Set pickup locations for shared food

Location data is used only in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers.

4. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to provide the foodsavvi service:

  • Managing your account and authentication
  • Displaying your fridge items, shopping lists, and orders
  • Connecting you with nearby food deals and community shares
  • Sending notifications about order updates and community claims

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Back4App (Parse Server) — cloud database and user authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Leaflet / OpenStreetMap — interactive maps. Privacy Policy
  • Nominatim (OpenStreetMap) — address lookup from coordinates. Privacy Policy

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your information is shared only:

  • When you share food in the community (your username and approximate pickup location are shown)
  • When you place an order (your username is shared with the business)
  • As required by law

7. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encrypted password storage (never stored in plain text)
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Authentication required for all personal data access

8. Data Retention

Your account data is retained until you delete your account. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile tab in the app. Upon deletion:

  • All personal data is permanently erased
  • Shopping lists, fridge items, orders, and community shares are removed
  • Wallet balance is forfeited (not refunded)

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data (contact us for requests)

10. Children's Privacy

foodsavvi is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.
